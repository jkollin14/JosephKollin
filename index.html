About Me

My name is Joe Kollin and I am a senior computer science major at the University of Cincinnati.

I will be graduating this upcoming April(2018) and will be going on to work for GE Aviation as a digital technologist.

When I am not in school or working for GE, I run my own company, Syntonic LLC. Syntonic is a technology consulting company which I have been running with two of my friends for the past two years.

Working for Syntonic has provided me an abundance of technical experience and a deep understanding of design principles. A link has been included to the Syntonic site in the activities section.

Experience

GE Business Analyst

During my second year of college, I worked for GE Aviation as a business analyst. The purpose of my rotation was to provide insight into the amount of money that was spent maintaining and hosting applications in their portfolio.

My rotation introduced me to scripting, application infrastructure, and working in a professional setting.

GE Infrastructure Developer

During my third year of college, I worked for GE Aviation as an infrastructure developer. Over the course of the four month rotation, I successfully migrated three applications to the cloud, built a POC, and helped redesign GE Aviation's CICD pipeline.

This rotation introduced me to several concepts in software development including development pipelines, git workflow, and client frameworks.

GE Software Developer

During my fourth year of college, I worked for GE Aviation as a software developer. During this rotation, I successfully designed and implemented an open office seating application. I helped design and create a RESTful API in Golang, integrated the API with Amazon's DynamoDB, and developed a Polymer client and server.

I presented my application to the CIO of GE Aviation at the end of my internship and it was optioned to be a cross-business application (All GE businesses would use the application)
